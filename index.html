QuantEdge: Investment App (Ongoing)

Language Used: Java, JavaFX

QuantEdge is an investment management app designed to help users track and optimize their stock portfolios with ease. It enables users to add initial investments, monitor stock performance, and gain insights into their portfolio over time.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive Portfolio Management: Easily add, edit, and view stock holdings with intuitive tracking tools.
  • Real-Time Stock Data: Integrated APIs provide the latest stock prices for accurate portfolio valuation.
  • Data Visualization: Interactive graphs and charts display investment performance trends, supporting informed decision-making.
